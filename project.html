Launching the headset that changed the course of virtual reality forever

In 2019, Oculus released the first device that would give virtual reality a shot at mass adoption: the all-in-one Quest. We created the campaign platform that would introduce it to the world. The campaign asked and answered a simple question – who wouldn’t want this version of virtual reality? Reality, obviously.

While developing the OOH, I created a headline construct to pay off the Defy Reality tagline when the physical embodiment couldn’t be present. This became the basis for the launch film and social posts, created by Lonnie Lind-Elliot, Andreas Ekelund, Sarah Litwin-Schmid, and Nicole Birch, all of whom made it their own.
