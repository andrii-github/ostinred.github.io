Is your business (and are you...) ready for your first electric vehicle? Bussiness | 12/30/'19
  • Electric vehicles are here to stay. And while Tesla’s #cybrtruck may not be your fantasy pick-up and Rivian or Bolinger are un- affordable for you, more options are coming online in the next couple of years that will change this dynamic and require your attention.
  • EVs change the financial dynamic of your business. Vehicle costs will be somewhat higher for a few years but the vehicles will have lower maintenance costs, less downtime and longer lifetime.
  • Your current vehicles however, will depreciate quicker and be phased out from primary markets within the next five to seven years.
  • You are very unlikely to drive your new 2020 gasoline or diesel truck in 2025 or 2027 at the latest.

Every time get online, one or another announcement about EVs (Electric Vehicles) pops up. Another EV startup got additional funding or another lost everything and shut down. In the mix of these messages, a few notes start covering light commercial vehicles are joining the news.

With focus on small and mid size businesses, what out specialists team at moshn is looking for, is the combination of choice, affordability and functional vehicles.

The market overall is warming up to Vans as a serious alternative to pick up trucks. What our team is looking for are more manufacturers to deliver.
