Founded by a collection of leaders who aim to bridge the gap between media, advertising and technology, Digital Future Council hosts quarterly events across the globe, with each event centered around the impact of a different emerging technology. Events are hosted in beautiful and hidden settings, that ignite meaningful debates and conversations. Together, we formulate tangible advice, and craft best practices for the global marketing and advertising communities when integrating new technologies such as AI, Big Data, and blockchain.
Cannes
Where media, marketing, and blockchain hang by the pool
18 & 19 June 2018 Cannes, France
coming soon
coming soon
Located in the heart of Cannes, on top of a secluded hill, sits our beautiful villa that boasts unmissable views and renaissance architecture to match. Across the 18th and 19th of June, we will gather creative leaders, academics and technologists under one roof to explore the opportunities and threats posed by blockchain technology, and how it might impact audience interaction, distribution of content, and monetization, in addition to advising creative leaders on blockchain’s role in empowering consumers to understand the true value of their data, particularly as it pertains to advertising dollars. That's not all, in between guests will enjoy healthy and yoga mornings, poolside parties and networking over a glass of rosé.
